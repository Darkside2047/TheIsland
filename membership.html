Serving a Diverse Range of Local Businesses

The Pleasure Island Chamber of Commerce is proud to have over 200 members today and that number is growing every month. Our members represent a wide range of businesses serving the communities of Carolina Beach, Kure Beach, Fort Fisher, Monkey Junction, Wilmington, and more. Search our site for Where to Stay, Where to Play, Where to Eat and explore Local Businesses to find restaurants, shopping, hotels, motels, vacation rentals, oceanfront condos, golfing, places of interest, and things to do in the area, as well as a multitude of services such as real estate, home building, financial planning, mortgage loan officers, photography, design, fitness centers, beauty salons, and boat rentals among many, many others.

Membership

Our mission is to:

  • Always serve our Members by actively promoting their businesses.
  • Enhance the economic vitality of southeast North Carolina through focused leadership and responsible representation
  • Create a community-wide resource for Members to network, exchange ideas and, ultimately, enhance their profitability
  • Serve as Ombudsman for Member businesses in addressing their issues, concerns and needs
  • Help make Pleasure Island, and southeast North Carolina, the best place to Live, Work and Play
